Customer Support Performance Dashboard

Problem: No centralized way to track support performance.

Tools: Power BI, Excel, DAX

The Customer Support Performance Dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of key service metrics, focusing on response efficiency, resolution effectiveness, and customer satisfaction. Helix Superstore Sales Performance Dashboard

Problem: Helix Superstore needed a clear way to monitor sales performance, understand customer purchasing behavior, and identify which products, cities, payment methods, and time periods were driving revenue.

Tools: Power BI, Excel, DAX

The Helix Superstore Sales Performance Dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of key sales metrics, focusing on revenue generation, customer behavior, and product performance. The dashboard highlights critical KPIs such as total sales, total transactions, total quantity sold, average rating, and average sales per transaction.

It further analyzes sales trends over time, product line performance, payment methods, customer segments, city performance, and sales by time of day. The dashboard helps identify top-performing product categories, peak sales periods, customer purchasing patterns, and opportunities to improve overall sales strategy.
